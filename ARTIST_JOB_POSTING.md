## Polycount Version

**Post in:** https://polycount.com/categories/freelance-job-postings

**Title:** [PAID] 3D Character & Equipment Artist — Stylized RPG (PS2-era JRPG Aesthetic)

**Body:**

Hi! We're looking for a **freelance 3D artist** to create character equipment and armor sets for an indie multiplayer dungeon-crawling RPG built with Babylon.js.

### Art Style

We're going for a **PS2-era JRPG aesthetic** — stylized but not cartoony, with detailed armor and equipment designs. Think of the sweet spot between hand-painted and PBR.

**Game references:**
- Final Fantasy XI (character equipment/armor silhouettes)
- Valkyrie Profile II (ornate but readable armor detail)
- Shadow of the Colossus (grounded, weighty feel)
- Resident Evil 4 (practical gear with personality)

**Armor/equipment style:**
- Sword Art Online (anime-influenced armor design — layered plates, elegant lines)

**Color palette:**
- Last Exile (muted, desaturated, grey-toned)
- Kino's Journey (earthy, subdued, atmospheric)

**Reference folder:** https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/folders/1siWWA5EXUOJi3gBGM8wCUGOcmo8AbAjB

### What We Need

- Modular armor/equipment sets (helmets, chest pieces, gloves, boots, etc.)
- Weapons (swords, bows, staves, shields, daggers — variety of types)
- Game-ready models optimized for real-time rendering (Babylon.js)
- Consistent style that fits alongside existing Quaternius-derived character models
- Rigging compatibility is a plus but not required for equipment pieces

### Technical Requirements

- Blender preferred (our pipeline is Blender → GLTF/GLB → Babylon.js)
- Reasonable poly counts for real-time web rendering
- PBR or hand-painted textures (or a blend of both)
- Delivered as .glb or .gltf files

### About the Project

Speed Dungeon is a cooperative/competitive multiplayer turn-based RPG with 3D graphics. Players race through dungeon floors, equip loot, and fight monsters. The game runs in the browser and as an Electron desktop app.

- Play the live version: https://roguelikeracing.com
- Discord: https://discord.gg/NxzPFUBVVm

### Compensation

Paid per asset or per set — happy to discuss rates based on your experience. Open to both one-off commissions and ongoing collaboration.

### How to Apply

Reply here or DM with:
1. Your portfolio link
2. A few pieces that you think match the style described above
3. Your rough rates or preferred pricing structure
4. Your availability
